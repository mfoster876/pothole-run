# Jamaica Environment Design Doc (the owner-provided reference)

> Provided by the owner on 2026-06-25 from real media. Canon reference for topography,
> road types, traffic and props. Feeds stage palettes, scenery, traffic and hazards.

## Gameplay intent (his words)
Dodge, aggressively: people J-walking, dogs, cats, potholes. Big **Mercedes yellow
JUTC buses** pass on the **left** lane; **taxis fly past and shake the cart with the
gust of wind trailing behind them**. Traffic flows on the **LEFT** (Jamaica).

## 1. Topography & biomes
- **Coastal edges:** sharp land→sea transitions; turquoise shallows → deep blue;
  resilient vegetation creeping right up to the coastal barriers.
- **Mountainous interior:** rolling hills + steep towering ranges, completely
  blanketed in thick dense tropical forest (not rocky/barren).
- **Atmosphere/weather:** dynamic skies — bright blue on the coast → heavy low cloud,
  moody overcast in the hills; **mist over the mountain peaks** is a distinct feature.

## 2. Road network (three types)
- **Modern toll highway:** smooth dark asphalt, bright white dashed dividers; sweeping
  curves with elevation change through hills; **metal guardrails** outer + **concrete
  Jersey barriers** in the median; dense untamed jungle pushing up to the edge.
- **Arterial / local roads:** smooth two-lane (one each way), dashed white line; flat
  & straight in towns; **no heavy barriers**; lined with **large mature trees whose
  canopies tunnel over the road**; roadsides = dirt shoulders, low concrete residential
  walls, simple fences, utility poles.
- **Coastal roads:** standard asphalt with slight wear + **sand accumulation at the
  edges**; hug the shoreline; defining feature = **continuous thick white-painted
  concrete wall** between road and the ocean drop-off.

## 3. Assets & props
- **Toll plazas:** concrete-paved zones, yellow protective bollards, orange ticketing
  machines, red-and-white striped boom barriers, overhead LED lane signs (red X /
  green arrow).
- **Signage:** large **blue highway signs with white text** (e.g. "Cacoon Lane");
  occasional large modern corporate billboards set off the highway.
- **Vehicles:** modern sedans & SUVs (often rear-mounted spare tyre); **white "coaster"
  minibuses** (ubiquitous local transit); yellow Mercedes **JUTC buses**; route taxis.
- **Micro-detail:** windshields carry a bright **red/yellow/green square Jamaican motor
  vehicle registration sticker** in the corner.
